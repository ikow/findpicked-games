Tap the letters to unscramble the word
Race the clock — solve as many anagrams as you can.
  • Goal: Unscramble the jumbled letters to spell a real English word.
  • Play: Tap a letter tile to drop it into the next empty slot. Tap a filled slot to send the letter back.
  • Auto-check: When all slots are filled, your guess is checked instantly. Wrong answers shake — fix and try again.
  • Timer: Each word has a countdown (longer for harder words). Solve it before time runs out.
  • Score: Base points scale with difficulty. Faster solves earn a speed bonus.
  • Hint: Reveals one correct letter in place. You get 3 hints per game — using one halves that word's score.
  • Shuffle: Re-arrange the remaining tiles if you're stuck.
  • Skip: Move on to the next word for zero points.
  • Difficulty: Easy = 4–5 letters, Medium = 6–7 letters, Hard = 8+ letters. Choose before tapping New Game.
  • Game: 10 words per round. Beat your best!
