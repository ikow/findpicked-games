How to Play

  • Tap a letter to start, then tap adjacent letters (including diagonals) to build a word.
  • You can also drag across letters to trace a word in one motion.
  • Words must be at least 3 letters and you cannot use the same cell twice.
  • Tap Submit (or double-tap the last letter) to lock in a valid word.
  • You have 3 minutes — score as many words as you can before time runs out.
