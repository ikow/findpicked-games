How to Play

  1. Place your fleet. Choose a ship from the list, then click a square on Your Fleet to drop it. Press R or tap Rotate to flip between horizontal and vertical. Use Random to place them all instantly.
  2. Start the battle. Once all five ships are placed, click Start Battle.
  3. Take aim. Click a square on Enemy Waters to fire. A red ✕ is a hit, a gray dot is a miss.
  4. Trade fire. The AI fires back after each of your shots. It hunts randomly, then targets neighboring squares once it lands a hit.
  5. Win. Sink all five enemy ships — Carrier, Battleship, Cruiser, Submarine, Destroyer — before your fleet goes down. Fewer shots = better score.
