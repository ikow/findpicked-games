How to Play

Classic Jacks or Better video poker. You're dealt five cards — choose which ones to hold, then draw replacements for the rest. Winning hands pay based on the payout table above.

  • Set your bet (1–5 credits) and press Deal.
  • Tap a card to hold it (tap again to release).
  • Press Draw to replace the un-held cards.
  • Any winning hand pays out credits based on your bet.

Royal Flush bonus: betting max (5) boosts the Royal Flush from 250 to 4,000 credits.

Winning hands (low to high): Jacks or Better · Two Pair · Three of a Kind · Straight · Flush · Full House · Four of a Kind · Straight Flush · Royal Flush.

Start with 200 credits. Your best credit total is saved locally and can be submitted to the leaderboard.
