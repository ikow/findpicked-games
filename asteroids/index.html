Asteroids

Pilot your ship through a field of drifting rocks. Shoot to break them apart. Don't get hit.

  • Rotate with ← / → (or A / D). The ship turns in place.
  • Thrust with ↑ (or W) to accelerate forward.
  • Shoot with Space. Bullets travel a short distance and despawn.
  • Wrap — fly off any edge to reappear on the opposite side.
  • Asteroids split — large rocks break into mediums, mediums into smalls.
  • Scoring — small 100, medium 50, large 20.
  • Lives — 3 ships. Brief invulnerability after respawn.
  • Waves — clear the field and a larger wave arrives.
  • Pause with Space (or P) when not firing — actually use the pause button.
  • Touch — left cluster turns, right cluster thrusts and fires.
