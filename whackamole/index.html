Moles pop out of the holes — tap or click to bonk them before they duck back down.

Each regular bonk scores +1. Golden moles are worth +5 and sparkle, but they vanish quickly.

You have 30 seconds. Speed picks up as the clock ticks down — keep up if you can.

Missing a tap doesn't cost you points — just timing.
