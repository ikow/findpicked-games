Music Maker is a step sequencer. Build a 16-step beat pattern across 8 instruments.

  • Tap any cell to toggle a note on or off.
  • Press Play to loop the pattern. Adjust Tempo from 60 to 180 BPM.
  • Tap an instrument name on the left to mute or unmute that row.
  • Press Save Pattern to store your beat and submit it to the leaderboard. Higher beat counts score higher.
  • Play Again clears the grid for a fresh pattern.
  • Export copies your pattern as a shareable code to the clipboard.
  • Keyboard: Space to play or stop, R to randomize, C to clear.
