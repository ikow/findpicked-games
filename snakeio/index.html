Snake.io

Steer with the mouse, arrow keys, or drag. Eat dots to grow. Eat fallen snakes to grow fast. Don't hit another snake's body.

  • Steer your snake with the mouse, arrow keys, WASD, or by dragging.
  • Hold Space or tap BOOST to sprint — costs length over time.
  • Eat glowing dots to grow by one segment.
  • When any snake dies, it leaves food behind. Eat the remains to grow fast.
  • If your head hits another snake's body, you die.
  • Other snakes die the same way — steer to trap them.
  • Score = your length. Try to top the leaderboard.

Mouse / arrows / WASD to steer · Space to boost · P to pause
