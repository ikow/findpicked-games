FreeCell

  • Move all 52 cards to the four foundation piles, building up by suit from Ace to King.
  • Build down on the 8 tableau columns in alternating colors (red on black, black on red).
  • The 4 free cells at the top each hold one card. Use them as temporary parking spots.
  • Drag a card to move it, or tap once to select and again to drop.
  • Double-tap a card to send it to the free cells or foundation automatically.
  • Multi-card moves are allowed when enough free cells and empty columns are available.
  • Use Auto-finish when all remaining cards can be sent to the foundations.
