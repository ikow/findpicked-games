How to Play

  1. Fill every cell with a 0 or a 1. Tap a cell to cycle through empty → 0 → 1 → empty.
  2. No more than two of the same digit may appear next to each other, horizontally or vertically.
  3. Each row and each column must contain the same number of 0s and 1s.
  4. No two rows may be identical, and no two columns may be identical.
  5. Violations are highlighted in red as you play. Solve the whole grid to win — faster clears earn more points.
