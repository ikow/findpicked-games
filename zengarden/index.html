Zen Garden

Breathe. Rake. Place. Repeat.

How to Play

  • Pick a rake — fine, medium, or wide — and drag across the sand to carve patterns.
  • Tap Rock or Plant, then tap the garden to place one wherever feels right.
  • Smooth gently flattens a spot if you want to start a pattern over.
  • Undo removes the last thing you added. Clear resets the whole garden.
  • There is no score. There is no timer. Slow down and enjoy.
Your garden saves automatically and is here when you return.
