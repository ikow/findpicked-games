How to Play
  • Tap each dot in numerical order: 1 → 2 → 3 …
  • Every correct tap draws a line from the last dot.
  • Connect every dot to reveal the picture.
  • The timer starts when you tap dot 1 of the first picture and runs through all six.
  • Wrong tap? The board shakes — keep going to find the right dot.
  • Finish all 6 pictures to record your time on the leaderboard. Lower is better.
