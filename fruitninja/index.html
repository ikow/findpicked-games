Fruit Slash

Swipe to slice the flying fruit. Avoid the bombs. Don't let three fruits fall.

  • Drag with your finger or mouse to slice fruit mid-air
  • Each sliced fruit scores points; faster swipes feel better
  • Slice multiple fruits in one swipe for a combo bonus
  • Slicing a bomb instantly ends the game
  • You lose a life for every fruit that falls unsliced
  • Game ends after three missed fruits — beat your best!

Swipe on touch · drag with mouse · space to pause
