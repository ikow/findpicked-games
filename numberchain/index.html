How to Play

  1. Press on 1 and drag through adjacent cells (up, down, left, right — no diagonals).
  2. Your path must pass anchors in order: 1 → 2 → 3 and so on.
  3. Every cell in the grid must be visited exactly once.
  4. Drag back along your own path to undo a step.
  5. Clear all five puzzles as fast as you can — the timer runs from the first move of puzzle 1.
