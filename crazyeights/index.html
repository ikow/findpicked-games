How to Play
  • Each player is dealt 7 cards. One card is flipped to start the discard pile.
  • On your turn, play a card that matches either the suit or the rank of the top discard.
  • 8s are wild — you can play an 8 on any card, then choose the next suit.
  • If you can't play, tap the draw pile to take a card. If it's playable, you may play it right away.
  • If the draw pile is empty, the discard pile (except the top card) is shuffled back in.
  • First player to empty their hand wins the round.
  • The winner scores the point value of cards left in the opponent's hand.
  • Card values: 8 = 50 points, J/Q/K = 10, A = 1, all others = face value.
  • Play to 100 points across rounds — first to reach it wins the match.
