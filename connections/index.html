How to Play

Find groups of four items that share something in common.

  • Select four words and tap Submit to check if they form a group.
  • Find all four groups without making 4 mistakes to win!
  • Difficulty levels:
    Yellow — Straightforward
    Green — Moderate
    Blue — Tricky
    Purple — Devious
  • Use Shuffle to rearrange the board.
  • Each puzzle has exactly one correct solution.
