Geometry Dash

Tap, click, or press space to jump. Dodge spikes, clear gaps, and ride the rhythm.

  • Your cube auto-runs to the right — you only control the jump
  • Tap the board, click, or press Space / ↑ to jump
  • Hold the key to keep bouncing whenever you land
  • Red spikes kill on contact — time each jump to clear them
  • Leap gaps in the ground or you will fall into the void
  • Dark blocks can be landed on from above — side impact is fatal
  • The world speeds up the further you travel
  • One hit ends the run. Score is your distance travelled.
  • Press P to pause

Space · ↑ · Tap to jump · P to pause
