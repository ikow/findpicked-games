How to Play

  1. Choose a difficulty level: Easy (3x3), Medium (4x4), or Hard (5x5).
  2. A colorful image is generated and cut into jigsaw pieces.
  3. Drag pieces from the tray below onto the puzzle board.
  4. Pieces snap into place when dropped near the correct spot.
  5. Complete the puzzle as fast as you can for a higher score!
  6. Score = difficulty bonus divided by time taken.
