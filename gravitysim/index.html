Gravity Simulator

Click and drag in the canvas to launch a mass. Build a stable system — longer you keep bodies orbiting, higher you score.

  • Place a body — click on the canvas to drop a mass at that point.
  • Set velocity — click and drag, then release. The arrow shows initial direction and speed.
  • Presets — Binary Star and Solar System spawn a pre-built configuration.
  • Physics — every body attracts every other via Newtonian gravity. Close encounters slingshot; collisions merge.
  • Collision — bodies that touch merge. Mass and momentum are conserved; radius grows.
  • Escape — a body drifting far off-screen is lost to deep space.
  • Scoring — 1 point per simulated second while 2+ bodies remain interacting.
  • Game over — fewer than 2 bodies left.
  • Speed — slow time to 0.25× or fast-forward to 4× with the slider.
  • Trails — recent positions fade out behind each body so you can read orbits.
