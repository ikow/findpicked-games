Tap a gem, then a neighbor — or swipe.
  • The board is an 8×8 grid of six colored gems. You have 30 moves per game.
  • Tap a gem to select it, then tap an adjacent gem (up, down, left, right) to swap. You can also swipe a gem to swap it with its neighbor.
  • A swap only counts if it creates a line of three or more matching gems. Otherwise the swap reverts and no move is spent.
  • Matched gems disappear and gems above fall to fill the gap. New gems drop in from the top.
  • If falling gems form new matches, they chain as a cascade — each cascade level multiplies your points (×2, ×3, ×4…).
  • Bigger matches score more: 3-match = 30 pts, 4-match = 60 pts, 5-match = 100 pts (before multiplier).
  • Keyboard: arrow keys move the selection, Space/Enter selects or swaps, R resets.
