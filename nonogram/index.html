Click cells to fill them — match every clue to win.

How to Play

  1. Each row and column has clues showing groups of filled cells, in order. A clue of 3 1 means a group of three filled cells, a gap of at least one, then a group of one.
  2. On desktop, left-click to fill a cell and right-click to mark a cell you know must be empty with an ✕.
  3. On touch devices, switch between Fill and Mark ✕ mode, then tap cells.
  4. Tap a filled cell again to clear it. Tap an ✕ again to remove it.
  5. You win when every filled cell matches the hidden picture. The timer tracks your best time per puzzle, and completing new puzzles grows your score on the global leaderboard.
