How to Play

  1. On your turn, tap an empty line between two adjacent dots to draw it.
  2. When you draw the fourth side of a box, you claim it and earn a point.
  3. Completing a box gives you another turn. Chain captures whenever you can.
  4. If your move does not complete a box, play passes to the AI.
  5. When every box is claimed, whoever has more boxes wins.
  6. Tip: avoid drawing the third side of a box — it hands your opponent a free point and sets up a chain.
