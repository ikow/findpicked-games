Spider Solitaire

  • Build descending sequences in the same suit, from King down to Ace, on the 10 columns.
  • When a full K-to-A sequence of the same suit is formed, it is removed from the board.
  • Remove all 8 complete sequences to win.
  • You can place any card onto a card one rank higher (suit doesn't matter for placement), but moving a group only works when those cards are already in descending same-suit order.
  • Any card may be placed on an empty column.
  • Tap Deal to draw 10 more cards — one onto each column. No empty columns allowed when dealing.
  • Drag and drop to move, or tap a card to select then tap a destination.
  • 1 Suit is easiest. 4 Suits is the classic challenge.
