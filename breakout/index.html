Breakout

Drag or use ← → to move. Break every brick to clear the level.

  • Move the paddle with ← → arrow keys, A/D, or drag on the board
  • Keep the ball alive — clear every brick to advance
  • Harder bricks take more hits and score more points
  • Each level is faster and has a new brick pattern
  • You start with 3 lives. Lose them all and the game ends
  • Press space to launch the ball or pause
Power-ups
  • W — Wider paddle for 12 seconds
  • M — Multi-ball (splits into three)
  • F — Fireball plows through bricks for 8 seconds
  • + — Extra life
