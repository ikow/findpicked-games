Mahjong

  • Match pairs of identical tiles to remove them from the board
  • A tile is "free" only when nothing sits on top and at least one of its left or right sides is open
  • Free tiles look bright; blocked tiles look faded — you can only match free ones
  • Flowers (4 unique designs) all match each other; the same goes for Seasons
  • Tap a tile to select it, then tap its match — tap the same tile to deselect
  • Use Hint when stuck — the next available pair will glow
  • Shuffle reorders the remaining tiles into a fresh solvable layout
  • Clear every tile to win — finish faster for a higher score
