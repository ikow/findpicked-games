Checkers

Classic Draughts — 8×8

Your turn

Goal. Capture all of your opponent's pieces, or leave them with no legal moves.

Moving. Pieces move one square diagonally forward to an empty dark square. Red moves up the board; Black moves down.

Capturing. Jump diagonally over an adjacent opponent piece onto the empty square beyond. If a capture is available, you must take it. When the same piece can keep capturing, you must continue the chain.

Kings. Reach the far row and your piece is crowned (marked with a gold ring). Kings move and capture diagonally in any direction.

How to play. Click one of your pieces to see its valid moves, then click a destination to move. Blue dots are moves; red dots are captures.
