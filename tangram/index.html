How to Play

Objective

  • Arrange all 7 Tangram pieces to match the target silhouette in the preview card.

Controls

  • Drag a piece to move it.
  • Tap / click a piece (without dragging) to rotate it 45 degrees.
  • Pieces snap into place when close to the correct position and rotation.

Difficulty

  • Easy — outlined guides show where each piece goes.
  • Hard — only the filled silhouette is shown.

Scoring

  • Score = shape difficulty / time x 1000. Fewer moves and less time earn a higher score.
