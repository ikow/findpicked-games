Hex

Connect your color from one side of the board to the other.

Blue (You)
0
Red (CPU)
0
  • Goal: Build an unbroken chain of your color connecting your two edges.
  • Blue (you) wins by connecting the top edge to the bottom edge.
  • Red (CPU) wins by connecting the left edge to the right edge.
  • Each turn, tap any empty hex to place a stone. Stones never move.
  • Neighbors: each hex touches 6 others. A chain can weave any path as long as each stone touches the next.
  • Swap rule: after Blue's first move, Red may "swap" and claim that stone if it looks too strong. This keeps the game fair — Blue should open with a move that isn't too strong and isn't too weak.
  • Draws are impossible in Hex. Someone always connects.
  • Game over: first player to connect their two edges wins.
