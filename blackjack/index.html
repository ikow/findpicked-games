Blackjack

Beat the dealer to 21 — without going over.

  • Goal: Get a hand total closer to 21 than the dealer — without going over (busting).
  • Card values: Number cards = face value. J/Q/K = 10. Ace = 1 or 11 (whichever helps).
  • Place a bet using the chip buttons, then press Deal.
  • Hit takes another card. Stand ends your turn.
  • Double Down doubles your bet, takes exactly one card, then stands.
  • Split a pair into two hands (separate bet equal to the original).
  • Blackjack (Ace + 10/J/Q/K on first two cards) pays 3:2.
  • Dealer hits on 16 or below, stands on 17 or above (stands on soft 17).
  • Run out of chips? Press Reset Bank to start again with 1000.
