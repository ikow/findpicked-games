      How to Play

      1. Each puzzle is a 5×5 grid of words that read both across and down.
      2. Tap a square to select it. Tap again to switch between across and down.
      3. Type a letter on your keyboard to fill the active square. The selection auto-advances.
      4. Use arrow keys to move, Backspace to delete, and Tab to flip direction.
      5. The matching clue stays highlighted at the bottom. Tap any clue to jump to that word.
      6. Hit Check Answers to flag wrong letters in red, or New Puzzle to load the next grid.
      7. Faster solves earn more points. Three puzzles ship with the game — beat them all.
