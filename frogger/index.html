  • Use arrow keys, WASD, or swipe to hop the frog.
  • Cross the road — don't get hit by a car or truck.
  • Cross the river by riding logs and turtles. Falling in or off the edge is fatal.
  • Watch out: some turtles dive underwater. Don't be on them when they do.
  • Reach one of the five home slots at the top to score. Fill all five to complete the level.
  • The timer resets each time you start a new frog. Beat the clock for a bonus.
  • Each level is faster. You have 3 lives. Good luck!
