Stack Tower

Tap to drop each block. Overhang gets trimmed — land perfectly to keep the full width.

  • A block slides side to side above the tower
  • Tap the board or press space to drop it
  • Any overhang past the block below gets sliced off and falls away
  • The next block inherits the trimmed width — keep landing clean to stay wide
  • A perfectly aligned drop restores a little width and counts as a combo
  • Miss completely and the tower ends — your height is your score
  • Blocks speed up the higher you climb
