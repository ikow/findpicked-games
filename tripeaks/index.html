Frequently Asked Questions

What is TriPeaks Solitaire?

TriPeaks (also called Tri Towers or Three Peaks) is a solitaire card game where 28 cards form three overlapping pyramids. Remove cards by matching one rank higher or lower than the waste pile card. The goal is to clear all cards from the tableau.

How does the streak bonus work?

Each consecutive card you remove without drawing from the stock pile increases your streak multiplier. The first card scores 100 points, the second 200, the third 300, and so on. Drawing from stock resets the streak to zero.

Can King connect to Ace?

Yes. In TriPeaks Solitaire, ranks wrap around. If the waste pile shows a King, you can play an Ace, and if it shows an Ace, you can play a King. This gives you more strategic options.
