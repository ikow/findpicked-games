Catch the Falling

Move the basket to catch fruit. Avoid bombs.

  • Move the basket with ← → arrow keys, A/D, or drag on the board
  • Catch fruit and coins to score points
  • Missing fruit costs a life — don't let any hit the ground
  • Catching a bomb ends the game instantly, so dodge them
  • Speed and spawn rate ramp up every level (every 500 points)
  • You start with 3 lives. Lose them all and the game ends
  • Press space or P to pause
Items
  • Apple — 10 points
  • Coin — 50 points
  • Bomb — game over if caught
Power-ups
  • W — Wider basket for 10 seconds
  • M — Magnet pulls fruit toward you for 8 seconds
  • S — Slow motion for 6 seconds
  • 2 — Double points for 8 seconds
  • + — Extra life
