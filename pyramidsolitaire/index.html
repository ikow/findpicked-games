Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pyramid Solitaire?

Pyramid Solitaire is a single-player card game where 28 cards are arranged in a 7-row pyramid. The goal is to remove all cards by pairing them so they sum to 13. Kings (value 13) are removed alone. Face cards have values: Queen=12, Jack=11, Ace=1.

What pairs sum to 13 in Pyramid Solitaire?

The valid pairs are: King (13, removed alone), Queen+Ace (12+1), Jack+2 (11+2), 10+3, 9+4, 8+5, 7+6. Only uncovered cards in the pyramid and the top card of the waste pile can be selected.
