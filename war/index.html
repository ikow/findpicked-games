  • Goal: Capture the entire 52-card deck. If the computer runs out of cards first, you win.
  • The deck is shuffled and split evenly — 26 cards each, face-down.
  • Press Flip to turn over the top card from each pile. Higher rank wins both cards, added to the winner's pile.
  • Ranks: 2 is lowest, Ace is highest. Suits don't matter.
  • War! On a tie, each side lays 3 cards face-down, then flips a 4th. Higher card takes all 10.
  • If a war happens with not enough cards to complete it, the player who can't lay down cards loses the game.
  • Toggle Auto to let rounds play quickly. Press Flip or Space to pause and resume manually.
  • First to hold all 52 cards wins. Your longest win streak is saved as your best.
