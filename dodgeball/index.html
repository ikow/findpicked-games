Dodgeball

Drag or use the arrow keys to dodge the balls. Survive as long as you can.

  • Move with arrow keys, WASD, or drag your finger across the board
  • Balls fly in from every edge — get hit and the game ends
  • Your score is how long you survive, in seconds
  • New waves arrive over time — more balls, faster balls
  • Press P or Space to pause
Power-ups
  • Shield — absorbs the next hit
  • Slow-mo — balls move at half speed for 5s
  • Clear — removes every ball on screen

Arrow keys · WASD · Drag · P to pause
