Goal: click the big button as many times as possible in 10 seconds.

Start: your first click starts the timer. The counter and progress bar show how much time is left.

CPS is clicks per second — your total clicks divided by 10. A casual human hits 5–7. Above 10 is quick. Above 12 is very quick.

Works with mouse, trackpad, or touch — one tap counts as one click. Holding the button down doesn't help; only distinct presses score.

Your best score is saved on this device.
