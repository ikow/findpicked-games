Connect Four

Drop four in a row to win

Red's turn

Players take turns dropping discs into one of the seven columns. Red moves first.

A disc falls to the lowest empty slot in the chosen column. Tap a column to drop your disc.

The first player to line up four discs in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — wins the game.

If the board fills up with no winner, the game is a draw.

Red wins!
