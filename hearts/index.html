Hearts

Avoid hearts and the Queen of Spades. First to 100 loses.

How to Play
  • Four players, 13 cards each. The goal is to score as few points as possible.
  • Each heart (♥) is worth 1 point. The Queen of Spades (Q♠) is worth 13 points.
  • Before each round, pass 3 cards: left, then right, then across, then hold (no pass), repeating.
  • The player holding the 2 of clubs leads the first trick by playing it.
  • You must follow the suit that was led if you can. If not, play any card.
  • The highest card of the suit led wins the trick. That player leads the next.
  • You cannot lead hearts until hearts have been "broken" (played on another suit).
  • No hearts or Q♠ may be discarded on the very first trick.
  • Shoot the Moon: take every heart and the Q♠ in one round — all other players get 26 points.
  • The game ends when any player reaches 100 points. Lowest score wins.
