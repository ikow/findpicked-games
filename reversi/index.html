Reversi

Flip your way to the bigger score on an 8×8 board.

Black (You)
2
White (CPU)
2
  • Goal: End the game with more discs of your color on the board than your opponent.
  • Start: Four discs are pre-placed in the center. Black moves first.
  • Placing a disc: You must place on an empty square so that one or more of the opponent's discs sit in a straight line (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) between your new disc and another of your discs.
  • Flipping: All opponent discs in that line flip to your color.
  • Valid moves are shown as dots. Tap one to place.
  • No moves? Your turn is skipped. If neither player can move, the game ends.
  • Game over: The player with the most discs wins. Ties are possible.
  • Choose vs Computer to play against the AI (you are Black), or 2 Players to share a device.
