How to Play

Solve as many arithmetic problems as you can — one at a time, against the clock.

  • Answer by typing or tapping the keypad. It auto-submits.
  • Timer per problem. Let it run out and the streak breaks.
  • Streak bonus: every 5 in a row bumps the level.
  • Levels widen the number range and add operations.
  • Modes: Basic (+ −), Core (+ − ×), All (+ − × ÷).
  • Division problems always have whole-number answers.
  • One wrong answer or timeout ends the run.
